Crossover

This project features a special crossover between Hong Kong Disneyland and b.delices, consisting of three unique series of merchandise. Each series uses a playful polka dot theme, designed to align with the charming style of b.delices while also celebrating the beloved characters of Disneyland. These exclusive packaging designs are available in limited quantities only at Hong Kong Disneyland, adding a touch of magic for visitors.
